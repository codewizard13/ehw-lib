droll: comical in an odd or whimsical manner.
Example: The Violator in the movie Spawn was a very droll, twisted demon.
Etymology: An interesting tidbit is that in Middle High German "trolle" (troll) is a valid origin for the word droll, and whereas one definition of trolle is "clown", then no wonder so many people are afraid of clowns!
